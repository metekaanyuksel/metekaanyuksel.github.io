## EEB313: Quantitative Methods in R for Biologists

I previously co-taught [EEB313](eeb313.github.io), the Quantitative Methods course in the Dept. of Ecology & Evolutionary Biology at the University of Toronto. Topics covered include data wrangling and visualization, likelihood-based estimation and inference, regression, and mathematical modeling.
